# Zamery Core V2

Use `assets/design-tokens.json` as the authority. The visual identity combines a lowercase bold `zamery` wordmark, subordinate `rooted in strength` tagline, Ink Navy structure, Root Terracotta action accents, Growth Teal learning cues, warm neutral fields, Arial typography, disciplined spacing, and consistent page furniture. Do not add a pictorial logo or mascot.

Branding must appear through at least the opening block and page furniture, with section accents where useful. Preserve meaning in grayscale with labels, borders, hierarchy, and non-color cues. Teacher surfaces lean on Ink Navy and Warm Sand; student prompts use Sky Mist and Growth Teal; Root Terracotta marks high-value decisions or cautions sparingly.
