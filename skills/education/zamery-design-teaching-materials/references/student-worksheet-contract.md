# Student Worksheet Contract

Design a printable form around the student's action. Expose progression with short section roles such as `Notice`, `Test the clue`, `Explain`, `Transfer`, and `Exit`. Prefer question blocks and ruled response areas over a giant data table.

Use a number rail no wider than 8% of usable width. A justified prompt/response grid uses 52–58% prompt and 34–40% response space. Allocate height from `assets/response-space-registry.csv`: selected response 8 mm, short answer 14 mm, explanation 28 mm, and two-sentence transfer 34 mm minimum. Keep exit items together and prevent a second page containing only one or two short rows. Student directions must sound natural and never reveal producer constraints.
