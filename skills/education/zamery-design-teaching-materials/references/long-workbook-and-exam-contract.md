# Long workbook and exam contract

Use a worksheet for a short classroom surface. Use a workbook for repeated practice across sections, and an exam pack for a graded administration.

Long workbooks require a cover/progress map, section openers, continuous item numbering, checkpoints, predictable continuation pages, and a final reflection. Start a new page at section changes and avoid a continuation page with only one or two short items.

Exam packs keep four surfaces separate: student exam paper, student answer sheet, teacher answer key, and teacher administration guide. All preserve item ID/version alignment. The exam paper never contains rationale, accepted answers, rubrics, or wrong-reason metadata. The administration guide records form identity, timing, permitted materials, accommodations, security, and incidents.

For 80–400+ items, generate documents from the approved form/bank rather than manually copying into a template. Verify first/last number, continuity, section counts, page/section starts, answer alignment, OOXML integrity, and every rendered page.
