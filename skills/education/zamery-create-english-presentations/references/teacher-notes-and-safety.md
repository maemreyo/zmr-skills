# Teacher Notes and Safety

Student slides may not contain `answer`, `answer_key`, `correct_answer`, `explanation`, `rubric`, or `teacher_notes`, including hidden notes, alt text, metadata, off-canvas objects, and animation labels. Put facilitation cues, expected answers, misconceptions, timing, and reveal guidance only in the separate teacher-note surface. A projected answer is allowed only as an intentional classroom reveal and must be represented as a separate, teacher-controlled state rather than hidden student metadata.
