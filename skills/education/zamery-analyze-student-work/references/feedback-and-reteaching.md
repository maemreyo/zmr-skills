# Feedback and Reteaching

Feedback names the successful move, the specific next move, and one actionable retry. Keep tone kind, direct, and age-appropriate; do not diagnose ability or motivation. Reteaching targets demonstrated errors or high-confidence misconceptions only. Choose the smallest move: contrastive example, short model, guided discrimination, pronunciation cue, evidence annotation, or one transfer retry. Do not regenerate a full pack unless the teacher asks.

When the diagnostic is persisted as an artifact, attach `artifact_id`, `artifact_type: student_work_analysis`, `audience: teacher`, positive `version`, `brief_id`, `grade_band`, optional `cefr`, `target_language`, `instruction_language`, `objective_ids`, `methodology_lineage`, redacted `source_references`, `authority`, `accessibility`, `brand`, and versioned `dependencies`. Never place raw PII in the envelope.
