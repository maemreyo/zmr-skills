# Education

English K–12 teaching skills: learner discovery, progress monitoring, long-horizon sequencing, lesson design, concept teaching, practice, assessments, reteaching, IELTS, video learning, materials, presentations, student-work analysis, and publishing.

Maintained in [`maemreyo/zmr-skills`](https://github.com/maemreyo/zmr-skills/tree/zmr-dev/skills/education) on the `zmr-dev` branch. Install with `npx skills add maemreyo/zmr-skills@zmr-dev --skill=<name>`.

## User-invoked

Reachable only when you type them (`disable-model-invocation: true`).

- **[zamery-teacher-copilot](./zamery-teacher-copilot/SKILL.md)** — Route broad, ambiguous, or multi-artifact English K–12 teaching requests to the right specialist skill. The entry point for the Zamery suite.

## Model-invoked

Model- or user-reachable (rich trigger phrasing so the model can reach for them).

- **[zamery-understand-learners](./zamery-understand-learners/SKILL.md)** — Build governed StudentCards, privacy-safe ClassProfiles, and teacher-approved Learner Context Snapshots before learner-sensitive planning.
- **[zamery-monitor-english-learning](./zamery-monitor-english-learning/SKILL.md)** — Aggregate dated evidence into objective-level trajectories and class progress profiles without prediction or intervention scoring.
- **[zamery-plan-english-reteaching](./zamery-plan-english-reteaching/SKILL.md)** — Plan the smallest evidence-grounded corrective move and a validated reteaching loop for downstream execution.
- **[zamery-design-english-learning-sequences](./zamery-design-english-learning-sequences/SKILL.md)** — Design versioned course, term, semester, or year-long objective, retrieval, assessment, and transfer maps.
- **[zamery-design-english-learning](./zamery-design-english-learning/SKILL.md)** — Plan, structure, sequence, or redesign an English K–12 lesson or unit blueprint before creating worksheets, assessments, or slides.
- **[zamery-teach-english-concepts](./zamery-teach-english-concepts/SKILL.md)** — Teach a vocabulary, grammar, pronunciation, reading, writing, listening, speaking, or language-use concept with memorable examples and comprehension checks.
- **[zamery-build-english-practice](./zamery-build-english-practice/SKILL.md)** — Create one-off worksheets, drills, homework sets, flashcards, reading passages, and differentiated student exercises.
- **[zamery-build-english-item-banks](./zamery-build-english-item-banks/SKILL.md)** — Build reusable 80–400+ item banks with deduplication, version control, source grounding, and JSONL/SQLite/CSV exports.
- **[zamery-compose-english-assessments](./zamery-compose-english-assessments/SKILL.md)** — Compose reproducible, blueprint-exact quizzes, tests, exams, forms A/B/C, and QTI exports from approved item banks.
- **[zamery-create-ielts-practice](./zamery-create-ielts-practice/SKILL.md)** — Generate IELTS Academic or General Training aligned Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking practice with official task-family constraints.
- **[zamery-build-video-learning](./zamery-build-video-learning/SKILL.md)** — Turn authorised video media into timestamped English-learning sequences with pre/during/post activities and H5P export.
- **[zamery-design-teaching-materials](./zamery-design-teaching-materials/SKILL.md)** — Compose approved content into branded worksheets, workbooks, exam papers, answer keys, and print-ready DOCX/PDF.
- **[zamery-create-english-presentations](./zamery-create-english-presentations/SKILL.md)** — Convert approved teaching content into classroom slides, decks, and speaker notes while preserving pedagogy.
- **[zamery-analyze-student-work](./zamery-analyze-student-work/SKILL.md)** — Mark, diagnose, and provide feedback on student responses, writing, and speaking submissions with PII redaction.
- **[zamery-review-publish-pack](./zamery-review-publish-pack/SKILL.md)** — Gate, finalise, package, and export teaching packs through seven quality gates before delivery.
