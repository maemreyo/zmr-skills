# Authority and labeling

Use official format vocabulary as a profile constraint, not as a certification mark. Generated content must carry one of `ielts_aligned_practice` or `ielts_aligned_mock`. Feedback carries `estimated_practice_feedback`.

Report objective practice results as raw correct answers and percentage. Raw-to-band thresholds may vary slightly between versions, and Academic and General Training Reading require distinct treatment. Only apply a conversion table when the teacher supplies an authorized, form-specific table with provenance; still label the result as an estimate.
