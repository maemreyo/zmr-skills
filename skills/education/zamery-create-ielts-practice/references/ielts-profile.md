# IELTS profile

IELTS has Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking. Listening and Speaking are shared across Academic and General Training; Reading and Writing differ.

| Section | Full format |
|---|---|
| Listening | 4 parts, 40 questions |
| Reading | 3 sections, 40 questions |
| Academic Writing | Task 1 visual description, at least 150 words; Task 2 essay, at least 250 words |
| General Training Writing | Task 1 letter, at least 150 words; Task 2 essay, at least 250 words |
| Speaking | Part 1 interview; Part 2 one-minute preparation and up-to-two-minute long turn; Part 3 abstract discussion; 11–14 minutes total |

Task 2 carries twice the Writing weight of Task 1. A partial practice activity may use fewer questions or tasks only when it declares `full_test: false` and does not visually imply a complete test.
