# Writing and Speaking feedback

Writing criteria are Task Achievement/Task Response, Coherence and Cohesion, Lexical Resource, and Grammatical Range and Accuracy. Speaking criteria are Fluency and Coherence, Lexical Resource, Grammatical Range and Accuracy, and Pronunciation.

For every criterion:

1. quote or precisely locate observable evidence from the response;
2. explain the effect on communication or task fulfilment;
3. give one concrete next step;
4. only then provide an optional half-band estimated range.

An AI-created range is practice feedback, not an official band. Do not estimate pronunciation from a written transcript alone; request audio or mark pronunciation as unassessed.
